Tic-tac-Yahooo (2 Players)

Welcome to the Mario vs. Bowser Tic-Tac-Yahoooo Showdown! In this exciting twist on the classic game, Mario and Bowser face off for ultimate control over the Mushroom Kingdom!

How to Play:

  1. Players:
    • Player 1 is represented by Mario
    • Player 2 is represented by Bowser
  2. Objective

    3. The goal is to get three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally using your character (Mario or Bowser). The first player to achieve this wins the game!

  3. Game Board:

    4. The Tic-Tac-Toe board is a 3x3 grid, just like the traditional version. However, instead of using circles (O) and crosses (X), Player 1 will place Mario's image in a square, and Player 2 will place Bowser's image in their chosen square.

  4. Turn Sequence:
    • Player 1 (Mario) goes first and selects a square to place Mario's image.
    • Player 2 (Bowser) then chooses a different square to place Bowser's image.
    • The game alternates between players until one player gets three of their images in a row or the board is full.
  5. Winning the Game:
    • If a player gets three Mario or three Bowser images in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal), they win!
    • If all squares are filled and no one has three in a row, it's a draw.
